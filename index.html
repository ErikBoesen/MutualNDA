Welcome to Pinky Promise, the Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) app tailored for everyday relationships and interactions.

In today’s connected world, your words and ideas are as precious as ever. Whether your brainstorming a new business, sharing personal stories, or discussing personal information, Pinky Promise helps you secure peace of mind by facilitating easy and enforceable mutual NDAs.

Our platform is designed to protect the confidentiality of your discussions, ensuring that trust is built right from the start. Join us and embrace the confidence to share freely, knowing your information is protected. Let’s make every conversation a promise kept.

QUESTIONNAIRE

Welcome to the Pinky Promise NDA customization survey!

As part of our commitment to craft NDAs that fit your specific needs, this survey is designed to understand the provisions you and all others involved in the NDA deem necessary.

Each party invited to this NDA will have the opportunity to fill out this survey. Based on the responses, the agreed-upon terms will be automatically written into your NDA. For any items that do not reach consensus, each party will be notified for negotiation purposes.

No NDA will be created without consensus on all elements, ensuring that everything you sign is something you agreed to!

Personal Secrets

Any personal secrets shared between the parties will be kept confidential.

Photos and Videos

All photos and videos shared during the term of this agreement are considered confidential.

Contact Information

Personal contact information such as phone numbers, email addresses, and physical addresses will not be disclosed to third parties.

Conversation Details

Details of all conversations held under this agreement will remain strictly confidential.

Other Confidential Information

The following additional secrets will be kept confidential:

Agreed Release of Information

This confidentiality agreement remains in effect until both parties agree to release each other from this commitment through a mutually executed written agreement.

Duration Until Death

The information disclosed under this agreement shall remain confidential until the death of the disclosing party.

Indefinite Confidentiality

This confidentiality agreement shall persist indefinitely, beyond the death of either party, safeguarding the disclosed information forever.

Public Domain Release

Confidentiality under this agreement will cease once the disclosed information enters the public domain through no fault of the receiving party.

Specific Time Period

The confidentiality obligations under this agreement shall last for [specify period] following the termination of their interaction.

In Case of a Legal Issue

Information may be disclosed if required by law or in response to a valid order of a court or other governmental body.

In Case of a Safety Concern

Information may be disclosed if it is deemed necessary to protect the safety of the individual or others.

With Mutual Consent

Information may be shared if all parties involved agree in writing to the sharing of specific details under agreed terms.

With Professionals Bound by Confidentiality

Information may be shared with professionals such as therapists or lawyers who are legally bound to keep the information confidential.

Never Share

No information will be disclosed under any circumstances, ensuring complete confidentiality.
